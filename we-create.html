Gem cutting is real art.

Properly chosen and well-made cut emphasizes and improves the properties of the stone, reveals its extraordinary beauty. Treating precious stones began in ancient times. Then it was more stone carving than a facet - various symbolic drawings and inscriptions were scratched on it.

The technique of this method has been kept in the strictest secret for a very long time, so it is still not certain when it was discovered exactly (according to some sources - in the 9th century, and according to others - in the XV).
